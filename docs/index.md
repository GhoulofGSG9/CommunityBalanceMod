# Community Balance Mod (CBM)

A balance and feature mod for **Natural Selection 2**, built by the community, for the
community. CBM retunes both teams, modernizes the AI, fixes long-standing vanilla bugs, and
heavily optimizes the engine for smoother gameplay.

New to CBM? This page explains the two editions and why they exist. The detailed change lists
live on their own pages.

![CBM](./assets/images/Global_Banner.webp "Community Balance Mod")

## Why two editions?

CBM does two different kinds of work:

1. **Foundation** &mdash; rebalancing, quality-of-life, bug fixes and performance optimization
   that improve the game *without* changing what's in it.
2. **New content** &mdash; brand-new units, structures, weapons and abilities that *expand* the
   game.

Not everyone wants both. Competitive players, purists, and server owners often want the fixes and
the better balance but prefer to keep classic NS2 exactly as they know it. Others want the full
expanded experience with new tools and strategies to explore.

So CBM ships in two editions. Both are built from the same project and share the **same
foundation** &mdash; the split is only about whether the new content is switched on. This way the
balance work reaches everyone, and players choose how much change they want.

## Content vs Core

| | **Content Edition** | **Core Edition** |
|---|---|---|
| Rebalancing, QoL, bug fixes, performance | ✅ | ✅ |
| Classic NS2 roster preserved | &mdash; | ✅ |
| New units, structures and weapons | ✅ | &mdash; |
| New alien support-structure upgrades & abilities | ✅ | &mdash; |
| Extra hive growth tier | ✅ | &mdash; |
| Alien armor upgrade | **Rage** (new) | **Carapace** (classic) |

**Content Edition** &mdash; the full, expanded game. Everything CBM adds, on top of the
foundation. For players who want the most variety and new things to master.

**Core Edition** &mdash; a faithful NS2 experience with the rough edges smoothed out. The same
balance and fixes, the classic roster kept intact (including **Carapace**), and nothing new to
learn.

Because both editions share the same foundation, balance and strategy carry over between them.
